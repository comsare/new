WE ARE AGAS LOGISTICS AND CONSTRUCTION LIMITED

AGAS Logistics and Construction Limited is a modern transport company focused on our customers wishes and needs for large capacity and flexibility, as well as the secure storage, handling, and transport of goods.

With approx. 25 employees diffused across Ghana and by using modern IT and a stable network of agents, we feel at ease in governing operations in Accra,Takoradi and Tarkwa on land.

All company divisions are administratively connected online. And you can book orders via the internet and use services such as our track & trace system.

AGAS Logistics and Construction Limited provides the best transport coverage in Ghana – on land – via our own system and through our partners, agents, and own networks.

We transport both a small parcel from Accra, Takoradi and Tarkwa. COMSARE Logistics and Construction Limited covers all of Ghana via our own high-quality network and a large number of circular-route lorries to ensure that our customers consignments arrive on time throughout Ghana.
